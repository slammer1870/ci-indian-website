Our Cuisine

Locally Sourced

All of our ingredients are fresh, organic and locally sourced Irish produce. Our philosophy is to bring fine dining to your door with the best ingredient available.

Authentic Influence

We take pride in our Indian heritage and seek to marry it beautifully with the art of fine dining. Our spices and ingredients come from traditional recipes, handed down from generation to generation, carefully crafted to satisfy the modern palette.

Fine Dining Experience

Brining the fine dining experience to your home is our commitment in the current times. Our dishes are curated to be transportable and easily served in the comfort of your own home.

Covid Policy

We have a strong commitment to our customers to be able to deliver fine dining at home. The core tenets of our Covid Policy are:

  • All food is prepared by staff that are socially distanced.
  • We solely accept cardless payment or online purchases.
  • Food collection is done via a one way system with limited number in any given area of the building.
  • Our delivery drivers wear masks and will keep 2 meters of distance when dropping off our food.
