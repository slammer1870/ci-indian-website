Covid Policy

We have a strong commitment to our customers to be able to deliver fine dining at home. The core tenets of our Covid Policy are:

  • All food is prepared by staff that are socially distanced.
  • We solely accept cardless payment or online purchases.
  • Food collection is done via a one way system with limited number in any given area of the building.
  • Our delivery drivers wear masks and will keep 2 meters of distance when dropping off our food.
